2019/2020 STUDENT REGISTRATION PROCEDURE

FOR FRESH AND RETURNING STUDENTS

Step 1 [How to login?]

  • Login with your JAMB Registration Number [Fresh Students only] OR Matric Number [Returning Students] as your Username and your Surname(In Uppercase) as Password.
  • In the case of changing your password, you will be required to provide your Email and Phone Number before proceeding.
  • In the case you where you have forgotten your password, click on the forgot password link on the bottom right of the login panel, you will be redirected to a different page where you will be asked to provide your Username, Registered Email address and New Password, click on the Reset Password button and you will see a pop up message that says "you are successful".

Step 2 [Student Biodata]

Returning Student are to skip this part of this section as they have already completed this step before, proceed to Step 3.

  • Click on Student Biodata

    For Fresh Students only

    • You are advised to fill in the the neccessary information in each section accordingly as it is compulsory. Under the Upload section, you will be required to upload the scanned copy of the following documents:
      • Passport Photograph (Not more than a month old),
      • Birth Certificate,
      • Certificate of Local Government of Origin,
      • Court Affidavit,
      • Attestation Letter,
      • JAMB Admission Letter,
      • O'Level Result(s),
      • NCE/HD/HND (For Direct Entry Students).
    • Click on "Finalize and Preview Biodata Registration" Under the Finalize section.
    • After clicking on "Finalize", your O'Level result(s) that you have supplied will be screened and if you passed, you will be asked to generate the invoice and go to any of the bank listed on your invoice to pay your school fee REMITA Platform only, otherwise contact the Live Chat below for assistance.
    • Note: You will not be able to generate the school invoice to make payment if you fail to properly fill in the sections or pass the online screening.

Step 3 [Payment]

  • Click on Payment.
  • Your payment due will be available as Debit under Student Ledger.
  • To make any payment, you have to select the payment, generate the invoice from which your RRR Number and Order ID will be made available.
  • Present the RRR Number on the invoice at any of the banks listed on the invoice and make your payment on REMITA PLATFORM only.
  • You have to confirm your payment under the "Confirm Payment Section" and print your Official receipt.

Step 4 [Accommodation (OPTIONAL)]

  • Click on Accommodation.
  • To be eligible for accommodation for accommodation application, you must have paid and confirmed your SCHOOL FEES.
  • Read the Hall Regulations and tick/check "I agree..."
  • Select the hall of your choice and click "APPLY NOW" and await an SMS of offer for Accommodation if accepted.
  • Note: For Student whose Phone Number are on DND , the SENDER ID for the offer of Accommodation will come as rUpdate and for those not on DND, it will come as FUTA ACMDATN
  • Follow the rest of the instruction afterwards to complete the Accommodation process.

Step 5 [Course Registration]

  • Click on Course Registration
  • To be eligible for course registration, you must have paid and confirmed your SCHOOL FEE
  • Your courses will be displayed for you to select appropriately, submit, print the pending course form and present to your Level Registration for approval (In the case of Fresh students). For Returning students, you are to submit, print the pending course form and present to your Level Registration Officer for approval.
  • After approval, you are to print the approved course form, present them for endorsement and signing and then submit to your department.
